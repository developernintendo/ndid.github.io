Please do not create duplicate accounts. Your organization administrator can create an account for you if your company has already been established. Your existing account may be referred to as an NDID or OMAS account.

Please contact support@noa.nintendo.com if you need help identifying the administrator for your organization.

Organization or Individual

Select 'Organization' if you are a company, non-profit organization, joint venture, partnership, or government organization. Select 'Individual' if you are an individual or single-person company. Selecting Organization will allow additional users to be established and roles assigned. Individuals are set up with all roles assigned to the registrant.
